Legendary underground city to the south east of [[Celestria (Country)]] in [[Valley of Frostheart]]. Contains one of the [[Horns of Malphos]]. Legend says that is has existed for thousands of years even before [[Malphos]]. It was a Faerie safe haven in times of crisis. [[Malphos]] attacked it during is wrat

It is also a location of a portal to the Fey Wild and houses Nebula Crown to seal the [[Malphos]].
